      5 Best Jungle Champions in Patch 10.13

      June 25, 2020, 19:16

      Although not many changes were made to Junglers this patch, here is a list of the top 5 Junglers in Patch 10.13

      5. Fiddlesticks

      Fiddlesticks continues his spooky rein of terror throughout the Jungle this patch too. Fiddlesticks is amazing after his rework, and looks set to perform well in a coordinated setting. Theres no mistaking his potency now, especially after the handful of buffs he got last patch. Anyone whos played him previously, should excel pretty quickly. Although he was not buffed or nerfed this Patch, he continues his position as a top tier Jungler.

      4. Ekko

      Ekko has always been an excellent jungler, largely thanks to his relatively quick clear, but also his ganking potential. His passive is fantastic, the self-shielding an stun from Parallel Convergence (W) amazing, and his burst from Phase Dive (E) eye-watering if hes fed. A beast and top of the pack (AGAIN) in Patch 10.13.

      3. Volibear

      Volibear has moved up the tier list this Patch for Jungleas many more players have understood how to play him now and there is a significant increase in his win-rate. His incredible turret dive potential has made him an even better Jungler as he can very easily gank any lane without the fear of dying to the enemy tower. He is much stronger as a Jungler than a Top Laner because as a Jungler, he is able to impact any lane with his Ultimate.

      2. Master Yi

      Who can forget our lovely Master Yi. Rocking at the top of the patch list as usual because of how simple he is yet powerful. His simplicity makes it a very easy pick in the low elo and his high damage output makes him a very easy pick in high elo. Together all the factors make Master Yi a win-win champion in all Elo’s.

      1. Nunu

      Nunu was one of the Junglers that got buffed this Patch and this buff definitely makes him a new top tier off-meta pick. League of Legends developers have buffed his AP Ratios in all his abilities giving him a viable new style of gameplay which can open up to more one-shotting his enemies while hidden in the bush.

      This patch did not affect Junglers much so most of the Top Tier champions in this Patch were also Top Tier in the previous patch. This is a good thing because you can continue to destroy everyone with the same top tier champions this patch too!
