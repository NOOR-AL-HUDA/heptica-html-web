      How to Use Special Characters in League of Legends?

      by Davis
      September 12, 2021

      On one level, gaming is all about being whatever and whoever you want to be. And League of Legends features over a hundred champions to play with and thousands of possible team combinations. Gamers tend to have a gamer-tag when they play various roles in so many different games. Double-lift and Faker are known for their gamer tags instead of their real names. For League, lets just refer to gamer tags as Summoner names.

      So what are some of the ways you can create cool special characters in League of Legends? 

      Also read: What does SS mean in League of Legends

      Using Special Characters in LoL Names

      You can simply go to the website LoL names (lolnames.gg) and check whether or not you Summoner name you are thinking of is available in the server you want to play in. Before choosing Special characters in League of Legends, its important to first assess the possibility of creating such special characters in the first place.

      You can simply choose the server from the drop-down menu and check the availability of the summoner name you want to adopt. The interesting thing about this name checker website is that it will never tell you the summoner name you are proposing is not available. It will always indicate the time when the summoner name is available.

      When you know the availability of the summoner name, a button will appear on the screen which says Add special characters. Click on that button.

      A new Tab will open on the browser window. A range of options will now be available on-screen showing the possible special characters available with the proposed Summoner name.
