      TOP 10 League of Legends Champions with the highest AFK ratio

      by Davis
      June 2, 2020

      We all know that some champion mains can be a little bit too much toxic. It’s time to find out which League of Legends champions have the highest AFK rate in the game. These stats are official and are taken from LeagueOfGraphs. So, without further ado, we’re presenting you the highest AFK champions!

      1) Yasuo – 1.45% AFK Rate

      2) Master Yi – 1.39% AFK Rate

      3) Xin Zhao – 1.28% AFK Rate

      4) Sett – 1.25% AFK Rate

      5) Rengar – 1.23% AFK Rate
