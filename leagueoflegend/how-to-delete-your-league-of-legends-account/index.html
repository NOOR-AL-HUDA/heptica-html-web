      How to Delete Your League of Legends Account?

      by Davis
      April 5, 2021

      There are different reasons why someone would want to either deactivate or delete their League of Legends account. Before you jump in and delete your Riot account, its important to know that this is a multi-game feature, so if you were to delete your Riot account, it is going to have an affect on any Riot game you have tied to it. 

      What this means is that the actions you make on the account could suspend or erase your account along with its associated progress for all of the games you have on the account. This sounds harsh, so we want to make sure you have all of the knowledge you need when it comes to deleting your League of Legends account. So, lets move forward and talk about deleting and deactivating accounts.

      Difference between Account Deactivation and Account Deletion

      Is there a difference between account deactivation and account deletion? Account deletion is going to permanently delete all of your personal information that is associated with that account. Before you delete it, you need to be 100% sure youre making the right decision because once you delete it, you will not be able to access your account again. Once the account is gone, you cannot change your mind because you wont be able to bring it back.

      Account deactivationis a bit different. When you choose to deactivate your account, access to the account will be restricted, but all of the data on that account will remain untouched. Basically, you wont be able to log into that account once its deactivated, but the information will stay on the Riot Games database. If you change your mind, you can simply request an account deactivation reversal. When it comes to an account deactivation, just remember that it wontprevent your rank from decaying.

      How to Deactivate League of Legends Account

      Before we tell you how to delete a League of Legends account, we first wanted to explain how to deactivate it. Before you jump in there and request to have your account deactivated, take note that it is perfectly fine to simply leave your account active, even if you dont plan on using it. By leaving your account active, it wont be deleted or harmed in any way. 

      To deactivate the account, you can submit a ticket to Riot Games Player Support and include the following information: 

      • Ticket Subject: Account Deactivation
      • Summoner Name (the name your friends see when youre in the game). 
      • Account Name (the name you log in to the League of Legends client with).
      • The creation date of your account. 
      • The server you play on.
      • The email address you used when you registered your account. 
      • The location you registered your account from 

      If you have any paying information on your account, then you should delete that before you deactivate the account. 

      Account Deletion

      Moving forward, we are going to discuss account deletion. 

      How long does it take to delete an LoL account?

      The deletion process will begin 30 days after Riot Games have received the deletion request. During this time, you will no longer have access to your account. Once the deletion process has started, it will be impossible for you to recover the account, so before you delete it, please be 100% sure you would like to have it deleted for good.

      If you just so happen to change your mind right after you confirm your deletion request, you can try to respond to the ticket as soon as possible to stop the deletion process, but this is not promised. If you do not let Riot Games know at least five days in advance of the deletion date, then they may not be able to save your Riot account. 

      If you would like to make a data request, make sure you do so before starting the deletion process. This is something that will need to be done in a different ticket. 

      Can you Use Your Information after the Account is Deleted?

      For thirty days following the deletion request, your username will still exist. All of the other information, such as your Summoner Name, Riot ID, and email address, will be inaccessible. 

      How do I get my Account Deleted?

      If you would like to permanently delete your League of Legends account, log in and send a ticket with the following information: 

      • Summoner Name (the name your friends see in the game).
      • Account Name (the name you use to log in to the LoL Client).
      • The original email address you used to register the account.
      • The location you registered the account from (City and Country). 
      • Have you purchased RP on the account? If so, list the payment methods you used. 

      What if I cannot Provide this Information?

      If, for some reason, you are unable to provide the information that is required, Riot Games may need additional information from you in regards to your account so that they can verify ownership. These steps are not taken to make your life complicated. They are taken to ensure the security of all accounts.

      You can speed up the process by sending a few emails to Riot Games Support center.

      Can I create a new account using the email I used on the old account?

      Once you delete the account, Riot Games will delete all of your information. So, to answer your question, yes, you will be able to use your previous email on the new account.

      Lets say I change my mind after 30 days, is there a way to recover my account?

      Unfortunately its not possible to recover your account after 30 days since all of your information will be deleted by then.

      Conclusion

      So now that you know how to delete your account, whether you delete it, deactivate it, or just let it sit there is entirely up to you. Honestly, what we would do is just let the account is there so that you can return anytime you like.
