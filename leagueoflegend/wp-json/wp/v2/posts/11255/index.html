Keyboards have undergone rapid changes over the years, with this giving a rise to low-profile keyboards. The main difference between a low-profile mechanical keyboard and a normal one is the height. With modification to various parts, the keyboard is able to significantly reduce its height which gives it a lower profile making it ergonomically friendly. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Low profile mechanical keyboards have a lot more benefits to offer than the standard ones. Firstly, due to their shorter heights, they are more ergonomic, allowing one to use them without suffering from fatigue on the wrist and arms. Due to their compact nature of shorter switches and slimmer keycaps, these keyboards are more portable and easier to carry around. Furthermore, the shorter switches reduce reaction time significantly when gaming, making the game more responsive. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

What is a low-profile mechanical keyboard?<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

A low-profile mechanical keyboard is a keyboard with a shorter body and shorter switches than a regular keyboard. The keyboard derives its name from these features and resembles keyboards on most laptops like Chromebooks and Macbooks. Its short height makes it more ergonomic and more portable making it easier to use and move around with. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
