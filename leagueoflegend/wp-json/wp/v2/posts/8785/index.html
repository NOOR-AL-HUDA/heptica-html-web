How to Pick a Gaming Keyboard?<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Before you spend your money on the first keyboard you come across, it’s important to know what you’re looking for. So here are some things to consider when buying your keyboard for League of Legends:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

1) Anti-Ghosting (Key roller)<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Anti-ghosting is a feature that allows for multiple keys to be pressed and registered at the same time. The regular keyboards are usually set to 1-2, which means that if you press 3 or more buttons at a time, the third won’t be processed at all. However, the gaming keyboards have the anti-ghosting set at 6 or more, so you can freely combine keybinds for the ultimate advantage.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Anti-ghosting can play a large role in League as well. For example, imagine hitting level 6 and deciding to all-in your opponent. You will need to level up your ultimate first (two buttons), then line up your combo (three buttons) almost at the same time. Without the right gaming keyboard, this is impossible to execute!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2) Switch Types<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

The switch is the mechanization system behind the key. In principle, there are two types: membrane and mechanical. The membrane ones are very common and found in the regular keyboards. But the mechanical switches offer much more stable support, and those are the ones we’re aiming for! Needless to say that they’re a must for League.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3) Build Quality<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Playing in stressful scenarios can cause you to lose control over your strength. And because we’re 100% immersed when we’re playing League of Legends, we don’t really know how hard we hit the buttons. This is why a quality keyboard is needed – to endure our rough tapping and to last us a long time.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

4) Backlighting<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Now, this one is personal, but better visibility leads to better performance overall. And, as gamers, we prefer dark rooms, so a keyboard that is well lit and clean can help us a lot!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
