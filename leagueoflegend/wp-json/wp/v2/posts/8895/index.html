How to Change the Language in League of Legends<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Having the ability to change the language in League of Legends is great. This game has a total of 11 servers with specific languages in them. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Some players aren\u2019t familiar with what this means. Basically, Riot assigns a particular server to every player. A localist (this is a word used to describe an individual playing from a supported region) won\u2019t find any issues. However, the player whose region language isn\u2019t listed on the server\u2019s list will get the short end of the stick if they don\u2019t know how to change the language in League of Legends. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

League of Legends can\u2019t actually change a language that isn\u2019t on their server\u2019s list. <\/strong>Earlier in the year, this is something that a player pointed out, and he received an honest response from the Riot Team. It stated that enabling language change in League of Legends is merely impossible. For some reason, it will infringe on the game\u2019s policy.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

There is a silver lining to this \u2013 developers managed to find a way to raise the restriction. Today, players are able to change the language settings.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

League of Legends Change Language <\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n
