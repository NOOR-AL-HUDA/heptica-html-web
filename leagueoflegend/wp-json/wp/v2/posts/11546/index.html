League of Legends is all about teamwork. If one of your teammates happens to be snowballing<\/a>, but your team coordination is weak, it\u2019s likely the opponent team will be able to pick your teammates one by one. Any single champion that is too fed in any team can only go so far without a team to back him or her up. And the key to winning team fights and battles is crowd control or CC<\/a>. While in the Champ select menu, be careful to have a balanced combination of mage\/tank and engage enemies with at least one champion with cc to win team fights.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What exactly is crowd control, by the way? Riot won\u2019t give you an official definition but what cc means roughly is the ability to control or limit the movement of your opponent\u2019s champions or disable or reduce the effects of their powers during team fights or in individual battles as well. Let\u2019s take a look at the most extended and also the most annoying (from the pov of opponents) CC abilities in League of Legends<\/a>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
