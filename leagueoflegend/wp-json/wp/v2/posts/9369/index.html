In League of Legends, any time you feel like doing so, you can change your name. However, you can only change your Summoner Name \u2013 as of right now, you cannot change your account name. So, when you pick your account name (if you haven\u2019t done so already), make sure you pick something that you don\u2019t mind sticking with for years to come. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

The Difference Between Account Name and Summoner Name<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The difference between your account name and summoner name is that the summoner’s name is what everyone sees you as \u2013 this is what appears when you\u2019re in the game. The account name, on the other hand, is the name you use to login to the League of Legends client. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
