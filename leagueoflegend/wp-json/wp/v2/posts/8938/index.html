If you wondered about the benefits of a good mouse, then let’s start with comfortability<\/strong>. Most gaming mouses are made with gamers in mind. That means that their design has been thought of beforehand and intended for long hours of gaming. Having a good and comfortable grip over your mouse is vital, especially for League of Legends. You want your mouse to be an extension of your arm, not something you’ll have to worry about during laning or team fighting.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The next thing is, of course, the response time<\/strong>. No matter how skilled or how fast you are in LoL, an excellent gaming mouse will double your potential. A mouse with a low response time will help you improve in dodging or aiming skill shots, as well as CSing successfully.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

And the last thing is the mouse buttons<\/strong>. These are put in that place for a reason – quick access to your most important spells and abilities. In LoL, you can bind your active items like Zhonya’s Hourglass or Blade of the Ruined King on them and be much faster in responding.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
