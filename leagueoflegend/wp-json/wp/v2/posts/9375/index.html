What is Champion Mastery?<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Starting out, we\u2019re going to explain champion mastery to you. Long story short, Champion mastery tracks the progression of a specific champion in terms of familiarity, skill, and experience. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

With champion mastery, there are a total of 7 mastery levels. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

You\u2019ll need certain items to craft a Mastery badge, such as Blue Essence<\/a>, Hextech Crafting, and Mastery tokens. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Each LoL player will start off with 0 mastery points. As you win games and progress, you will earn what is called champion points (CP), which will be added to your mastery level. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

The number of champion points you earn all depends on your performance in the game you played. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
