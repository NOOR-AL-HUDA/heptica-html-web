5 Strongest Mid Lane Champions in League of Legends Patch 10.10

Here are the 5 best mid lane champions in Patch 10.10. With these champions, you’ll be able to climb the ranked ladder with ease

Mid lane is the most popular lane in League of Legends. Mid laners have the ability to gank both the top lane and the bot lane without losing too much time and farm on their lane. Your team needs your help with drake? Cool, no problem. Your team needs help on Rift Herald/Baron Nashor? No problem as well. So, without further ado, here are the strongest mid lane champions in Patch 10.10 :

5. Annie

Annie got a massive buff on her Molten Shield (E) and Summon: Tibbers (R) abilities. She hasn’t been a popular pick in the last few patches, but we believe that her popularity will rise in this patch. Here’s the best Annie build

4. LeBlanc

I personally main LeBlanc and I’m glad that she’s not a popular champion because it’s really hard to play against her, especially if your team is squishy. I’ve had the best success with these runes on LeBlanc: Electrocute – Sudden Impact – Eyeball Collection – Ravenous Hunter – Presence of the Mind – Coup de Grace. If you’re interested in learning how to play LeBlanc, here’s a LeBlanc build guide from FuzzMonkey

3. Diana

Riot thought that Diana has too much health in the early game so they’ve decided to give her a nerf (Base Health 594 — 570). It seems that that nerf didn’t really affect Diana because she’s still one of the best picks for the mid lane. If placed right, her ultimate is a game-winner. You can play Diana in so many different ways, so here’s the best Diana build guide

2. Yasuo

I get it, you don’t like to see Yasuo in your games. I personally always ban him just because I don’t like seeing him in the game. There are a few players that can actually play Yasuo very well, but the ones that know how to play him can solo carry your ranked games. However, please don’t try to learn how to play Yasuo in ranked. He’s an easy champion to play, but hard to master. You should play him in normal games for a few months before trying him out in ranked.

1. Twisted Fate

Majority of League players think that Twisted Fate is not a good champion because he doesn’t have any escaping abilities. They’re right. He can get ganked easily, BUT, his ultimate makes up for that. He’s not a good champion for 1v1 lane fights. When you’re playing Twisted Fate you should always be looking how you’re going to help your bot lane or top lane. Whenever your ultimate is ready, you should always use it as soon as possible.

What are your thoughts on the best mid laners in 10.10? I truly think that, with these 5 mid lane champions, you can climb the ranked ladder without any problems.
