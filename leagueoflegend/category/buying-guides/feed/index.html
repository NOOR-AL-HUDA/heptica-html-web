Is 30 FPS Good for Webcam?

To make it simple, fps stands for frames per second. The frame rate can also be defined as the number of different frames your webcam can capture and view within a specific period of time.

Putting all this information together, what is a webcam that has a frame rate of 30 fps? Purchasing a webcam with a frame rate of 30 fps offers you a webcam capable of taking 30 pictures every second.

Nowadays, 30 fps is the bare minimum for webcams. If you can afford to increase your budget, the more expensive webcams can capture up to 120 fps at 1080p.

However, since 120 fps webcams might be unaffordable for some people, purchasing 30 fps or higher webcams can still offer you good quality and non-lagging video.

Keep in mind that a webcam with 60 fps doesn’t indicate that the webcam can capture 60 frames every second. How, you might ask?

Well, the camera resolution usually decides the quality of the sharpness in the video. As a result, the frame rates depend on the webcam’s resolution. For instance, we have two different webcams, one with 60 fps and the other one is 30 fps. So, both webcams work the same. Except for the webcam with 60 fps can capture 60 fps at 720p and capture 30 fps at 1080p.

Also, consider other different reasons that influence your webcam’s frame rate. These factors include your webcam’s software, the light condition in your recording space, and the speed of your internet connection. 

Here are some tips and tricks to improve your webcam’s frame rate:

Firstly, you won’t be able to take videos at a higher fps than the one installed in the webcam. However, the optimal frame rate happens in bright lighting conditions.

Lighting has a significant impact on the quality of your webcam. A $50 webcam can offer the same quality as a DSLR camera with the help of great lighting.

Secondly, running several other devices on your computer decreases the efficiency of the frame rate. It is recommended to use USB 3.0 ports for the webcam because they have the highest speeds. 

Finally, if you’re trying to customize any recording to different conditions or events, for example, if you will be streaming a game night with your friends on Zoom, then you probably shouldn’t send high HD videos. Save the high quality and demanding programs on your computer for necessary situations or that matter.

Thus, Ethernet switches can help here. The best Ethernet switches may be able to connect many devices to a single router without sacrificing speed. WiFi is excellent, but it’s no substitute for a traditional Ethernet connection. They are more reliable, smoother, and more durable. Ethernet switches are recommended to connect a TV, laptop, play video games, and other devices to a wired network.

Once you’ve connected one of the boxes (known as hubs, splitters, or switches) to a specific port on the router, your connection is ready. Then you can connect an Xbox, television, laptop, or just about any wired connection device to the router, and it will work as if it had been linked directly to it.

If you ever need to set the switch in a separate room from your router, you may use a set of powerline connectors to link the two. Although it will result in a weaker connection, it will allow you to avoid running a lengthy network cable.

Typically the switches are plug-and-play, also known as “unmanaged.” Therefore, there is no need to configure anything. As a fact, there isn’t much of a difference between various brands of unmanaged switches. However, minor variations may make another one more appealing than others, such as the positioning of the connector, the layout of the ports, or perhaps the display of the LEDs.

Price, guarantee, structure, and aesthetics play a role, though the latter is unlikely to be a top concern for many.

How to Pick a Gaming Keyboard?

Before you spend your money on the first keyboard you come across, it’s important to know what you’re looking for. So here are some things to consider when buying your keyboard for League of Legends:

1) Anti-Ghosting (Key roller)

Anti-ghosting is a feature that allows for multiple keys to be pressed and registered at the same time. The regular keyboards are usually set to 1-2, which means that if you press 3 or more buttons at a time, the third won’t be processed at all. However, the gaming keyboards have the anti-ghosting set at 6 or more, so you can freely combine keybinds for the ultimate advantage.

Anti-ghosting can play a large role in League as well. For example, imagine hitting level 6 and deciding to all-in your opponent. You will need to level up your ultimate first (two buttons), then line up your combo (three buttons) almost at the same time. Without the right gaming keyboard, this is impossible to execute!

2) Switch Types

The switch is the mechanization system behind the key. In principle, there are two types: membrane and mechanical. The membrane ones are very common and found in the regular keyboards. But the mechanical switches offer much more stable support, and those are the ones we’re aiming for! Needless to say that they’re a must for League.

3) Build Quality

Playing in stressful scenarios can cause you to lose control over your strength. And because we’re 100% immersed when we’re playing League of Legends, we don’t really know how hard we hit the buttons. This is why a quality keyboard is needed – to endure our rough tapping and to last us a long time.

4) Backlighting

Now, this one is personal, but better visibility leads to better performance overall. And, as gamers, we prefer dark rooms, so a keyboard that is well lit and clean can help us a lot!
