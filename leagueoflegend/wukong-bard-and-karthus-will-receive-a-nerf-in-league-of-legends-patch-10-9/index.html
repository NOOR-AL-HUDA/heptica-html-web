Wukong, Bard and Karthus will receive a nerf in League of Legends Patch 10.9

Since he got his rework, Wukong has become one of the most played champions on Summoners Rift. Riot just made him too strong, and now, once they’ve realised that he’s too strong, they decided to nerf him.

He will not be the only champion that will get hit by the nerf hammer on Wednesday, April 29. Riot has decided that they will also be nerfing a couple of other champions. Here are all the champions that will receive a nerf in the next League patch 10.9
