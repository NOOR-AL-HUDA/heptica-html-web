      How To Get Blue Essence Fast?| Blue Essence Farming

      November 21, 2020, 07:28

      If you are wondering how to get Blue Essence Fast, make sure you read this article to the end for all the tips and tricks!

      What is Blue Essence?

      Blue Essence is one of the main currencies players can accumulate in League of Legends. For players who are returning to League of Legends, think of Blue Essence as the new format of Influence Points.

      Currently, there are only two currencies that are considered primary in League of Legends, the other currency goes by the name Riot Points, however, players have to pay for Riot Points, unlike Blue Essence.

      What can we do with Blue Essence?

      • Purchase champions (through the Store, or with champions shards).
      • Purchase additional rune pages.
      • Upgrade a champion mastery to level 6 or 7.
      • Purchase chromas whenever they’re on sale.
      • Purchase exclusive content in the Essence Emporium.
      • Change your summoner name.

      How To Get Blue Essence Fast?

      The Fastest way ever to farm Blue Essence is through level ups. You get a champion capsule every time you level up and opening the champion capsule then gives you champions which you can disenchant for 90, 270, 630, 960, 1260 and 1560 Blue Essence depending on the cost of the Champion.

      Starting from Summoner level 5, champion capsules are awarded at level-ups and contain champion shards that can be disenchanted to obtain BE, activated to have a 7 days rental, or upgraded to permanently unlock the champion.
      These capsules additionally also have a probability of giving out Blue Essence between a range of 860 to 1260 Blue Essence.

      Well if you truly want to know the fastest way, it is to buy XP Boosts, and play ARAM’s.
      ARAM’s also have the additional feature of a boost which gives everyone a random skin for their champion and also an extra 200 Blue Essence for yourself. ARAM is also well known for farming XP and there are many different tricks to do them. Combining all of those with XP Boosts will truly be the fastest way of leveling up and thus getting more champion capsules which will then give you your desired Blue Essence.

      How Much Blue Essence Per Level?

      Every level after summoner level 5 gives you a champion capsule. Each champion capsule contains 1-3 champions, and can give an extra bonus Blue Essence.

      Champion capsules are very random and the amount of Blue Essence you get per Level greatly depends on it. You can get anywhere from 90 Blue Essence being the worst amount all the way up to 5040 Blue Essence assuming you get all the best rewards. Getting both of these are quite impossible so you should be averaging at about 1500 Blue Essence per level.

      There are also some milestone levels such as Level 30, Level 40, Level 50, Level 60, Level 75, Level 100, Level 125 and so on where you get a special capsule which gives you much more rewards.

      If you are still finding it hard to calculate, going from level 1 to level 30 gives about 40,000 Blue Essence. Now you may think going from level 1 to level 30 must be hard but League of Legends developers have made sure that leveling up is easy and you can level up in a few games.

      Blue Essence Farming – A Complete Guide

      Blue Essence Farming is very simple. It is where you devote all your resources to get Blue Essence which you can then instead use to purchase champions or other accessories from the shop.

      There are many different ways to get Blue Essence and this section will list you every single one of those Methods.

      1) Champion Capsule

      The first one being the Champion Capsule which you receive every time you level up. This Champion capsule contains Champions which can be disenchanted for Blue Essence between the range of 90-1560 Blue Essence. This is by far the simplest and easiest way to earn Blue Essence.

      Certain Milestone Levels also give an enhanced Champion Capsule which contains many more rewards and gives you more Blue Essence!

      2) First Win of The Day

      First Win of The Day is a mission that resets itself every 20 hours. This Mission gives you 50 Blue Essence for the first win of the day. Although not being much, you can earn 350 Blue Essence per week if you play 1 game a day and win it. This really adds up in the long term and not for quick farming of Blue Essence.

      3) Hextech Chests

      Chests have a 20% chance of giving you a champion so opening chests can also help you get Blue Essence. The good thing about Chests is that they will give you 960 Blue Essence for sure as Chests only give 4800 Blue Essence or higher costing champions.

      4) Missions

      League of Legends very often announces a certain set of Missions that can have Blue Essence as a reward. These are the missions that come along with Events such as The Project Event or the Pulsefire Events. These missions can give you Blue Essence anywhere from the range of 800 to 6300 which is quite the amount. Although this isn’t a reliable way of earning Blue Essence as there have been many events where Blue Essence wasn’t a part of their rewards.

      5) Event Tokens

      Event Tokens can also be a great way of farming Blue Essence as 1 Event Tokens is equal to 10 Blue Essence or 10 Event Tokens is equal to 100 Blue Essence. Assuming you buy the Event Pass and gather about 2000 tokens, you can get 20,000 Blue Essence. Now, this is a real way of Farming Blue Essence.

      6) Honor Capsules

      Honor Capsules also have chances of giving Blue Essence as they give out Champion Shards and Emotes which can be disenchanted for Blue Essence. You receive an Honor Capsule every time you hit a milestone or increase in Honor Level. Per season a Summoner can only get a total of 12 Honor Capsules for the 3 Milestones per level and 1 level up.

      These are all the ways to get Blue Essence in League of Legends and now you can decide which is the best way for you.
