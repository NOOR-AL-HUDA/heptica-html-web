      How To Check How Many League Games Have You Played?

      by Davis
      August 26, 2021

      Ever wondered how many League of Legends games you have played? For whatever reason, surely you have! Well, if you still dont have no idea how to check the total number of played games, continue reading this article as Ill show you some simple tricks on how you can check it by yourself without downloading any programs!

      Table of Content show
      How to Check How many Ranked LoL Games you have Played
      How to Check How Many Normal LoL Games you have played
      How Many Hours Have you Spent Playing LoL?
      Final Word

      How to Check How many Ranked LoL Games you have Played

      Theres a difference between ranked LoL games and normal LoL games. Finding how many ranked LoL games you have played is much easier as it is built into the tab. On the stats tab, when youre logged into your account, you can see how many games you have played in total, and all game modes are separate, making it even easier for you. 

      How to Check How Many Normal LoL Games you have played

      To find how many normal LoL games you have played, its going to take a bit more work on your end. 

      Follow these steps

      • Step one: Go to op.gg (this is a website where youll be putting your summoner name in to see how many LoL games you have players
      • Step two: Click on the Champions tab on the site. 
      • Step three: Click on normal.

      And there you go, like magic, youll see the amount of normal League games you have played. Did the number surprise you? 

      How Many Hours Have you Spent Playing LoL?

      Now that you know how many LoL games you have played since you started, wouldnt it be cool to see how many hours youve spent on the game? You can do this too! Theres a site called wol.gg (go there). The WOL stands for Wasted on League, but in our book, when you play LoL, youre not wasting any time at all. 

      Basically, long story short, as long as youre having fun, time isnt being wasted. Anyhow, youll want to go to WOL.GG, and from there, enter in your summoners name as you did at OP.GG. Follow those steps, and youll get the amount of hours you have spent playing this exciting game. 

      Warning, the hours do add up, so you may end up being surprised. When I looked at my favorite game to see how many hours I played, I found that I had put in a total of 1,421 hours, while a friend of mine only put in 82 hours. Obviously, what this means is that I really love the game. 

      So, if you have found that you have put a lot more time into playing LoL than you have, per se, living life, what this means is that you have found something that really excites you and has captured your interest, and that is hard for some people to find in life, so congratulations on that. 

      Final Word

      When you discovered how many League games you have played, how did that make you feel? How about how many hours of LoL you have played? Dont worry, I have a friend who played over 2,248 hours of League, and to this day, he still continues to play it, and I dont see anything wrong with this. 

      As long as youre still living your life, making a living, having dinner, and keeping your home clean, then you should be able to put your time wherever you choose, and if LoL is where you choose to put your time to, then that is perfectly fine. In the end, what matters the most is that youre having fun! Plus, if youre putting that much time into something, youre obviously becoming an expert. 

      So, how many League of Legends experts do I have reading this blog right now? Do you consider yourself an expert in LoL?
