5 Reasons Why Lux and Ezreal Are the Perfect Bot Lane Combo

October 1, 2020, 23:10

League of Legends has its fair share of combos, strategies, and yes, love pairs. Ever since the game came out, fans have speculated about certain champions being made to complement one another. We are talking about spells, strengths, styles, appearances, and lore – everything needs to match for a relationship to exist! And while there are a lot of “guessed at” LoL couples, the most popular one is by far Lux and Ezreal!

At first sight, everyone might agree that these two form the best combo in the game. It is because everything about them just “clicks” together. Lux benefits a lot from Ezreal’s playstyle and Ezreal from Lux’s in return. They play out similarly, and given in the right hands; they can win every matchup!

Throughout the years, players have often been outspoken about their relationship outside of the game. These speculations are based on famous hints that could be found both in-game and in the official lore from Riot Games. Everything about Lux and Ezreal seems pretty flawless, and we have no reason to believe that they won’t end up happily ever after! But, the reality isn’t so clear…

There are probably millions of reasons why Lux and Ezreal make the best combo in the League of Legends universe, and one can talk for hours about why Riot made them so! But, let’s focus on the five most obvious ones and give answers to why Lux and Ezreal are the biggest fan-favorite couple of all! Beginning, of course, with their synergy?

5. The Bot Lane Synergy of Lux and Ezreal

Given in the right hands, Lux and Ezreal can be a real terror in the bot lane. Experienced players know how to abuse their long-range to poke and secure kills, without even taking much damage. Not many bot lane combos can match Ezreal and Lux’s prolonged fights, simply because they outrange and outdamage most of their opponents. 

But it’s not only the long-range and damage potential of Ezreal and Lux that makes them such a feared pair in the bot lane but their synergy too. All of Lux’s abilities go extremely well with all of Ezreal’s abilities. Both of them are poky mages who like to hit you without getting hit in return. 

Ezreal doesn’t have any crowd control abilities. So, Lux comes handy for him because she brings a root with her Q – Light Binding, which works on two targets, and a slow through her E – Lucent Singularity. This is perfect because it makes it so much easier for Ezreal to hit all of his skill shots, like the W – Essence Flux and Q – Mystic Shot combo. Lux is also able to follow Ezreal with her shield whenever he jumps forward because it also has a massive range!

But, perhaps their main strength is their big burst with their ultimates – Final Spark and Trueshot Barrage. If Lux manages to put an enemy champion in a binding, then she and Ezreal can drop their Rs to delete that champion from the game instantly! Their combined damage can’t be survived in the bot lane, especially if they start winning early.

4. Lux and Ezreal Have Insane Damage Together

While Ezreal starts off slowly and becomes a monster in the late game, Lux has consistent damage throughout it. But both of them scale awfully well, and with the right builds, they are truly unstoppable! These are the best runes and item choices for both of them.

Best build and runes for Lux

Runes:

Resolve

  • Guardian
  • Shield Bash
  • Bone Plating
  • Revitalize

Sorcery

  • Nimbus Cloak
  • Transcendence

Bonuses

  • Adaptive Force
  • Adaptive Force
  • Armor

While these are the most favored runes by pros, in reality, Lux is a very versatile champion and can be played with many different keystones. Things like Arcane Comet, Summon Aery, Glacial Augment, Electrocute, and even Dark Harvest can all be taken, depending on your style and preference. But the Guardian rune page is the best for being a proper support with Lux because it gives a fantastic amount of shielding and peeling potential, which is excellent for Ezreal, who, in return, doesn’t have anything defensive except Heal.

Items:

  • Spellthief’s Edge / Shard of True Ice
  • Athene’s Unholy Grail
  • Ardent Censer
  • Redemption
  • Rabadon’s Deathcap
  • Ionian Boots of Lucidity

As with the runes, you can always mix and match things on Lux. Full AP build can also work, so going for items like Morellonomicon, Void Staff, or Zhonya’s Hourglass can be beneficial if you need some extra damage in your team. However, if Ezreal is going to carry the game, then Lux should go for many supportive items like Mikael’s Crucible and Locket of the Iron Solari.

Best build and runes for Ezreal

Runes:

Precision

  • Conqueror
  • Presence of Mind
  • Legend: Bloodline
  • Cut Down

Inspiration

  • Biscuit Delivery
  • Cosmic Insight

Bonuses

  • Attack Speed
  • Adaptive force
  • Armor

After the removal of Kleptomancy, Conqueror is the best keystone for Ezreal, and it never changes. The secondary runes can be shuffled, especially with the Sorcery tree, if more damage and mana is what you want.

Items:

  • Manamune / Muramana
  • Iceborn Gauntlet / Trinity Force
  • Death’s Dance
  • Blade of the Ruined Kings
  • Mortal Reminder
  • Ionian Boots of Lucidity

Parts of Ezreal’s build depends on the enemy ADC. For example, if he faces many ranged champions, Ezreal needs to buy Trinity Force; but against melee champions, Iceborn Gauntlet is much better. So, items like Guardian Angel, Quicksilver Sash, and Bloodthirster can be used to carry to victory.

If Lux and Ezreal manage to get to full build, then the game can be decided by those two. It would undoubtedly be extremely difficult for the enemies to kill even one of them because they will have a ton of shields, slows, roots, and damage to overpower their attacker. Playing the Lux and Ezreal combo right can take a bit of practice, but it’s worth every second of it!

3. Lux and Ezreal Have Similar Appearances and Skins

Purely for the pleasure of the eyes, there are no other two champions that go better together than Lux and Ezreal! As the Lady of Luminosity, all of Lux’s spells descend from the light. Their main theme is white/yellow, and Ezreal is similar, along with his Shuriman gauntlet. His abilities are also yellow, with white hues here and there.

The case is the same with all of their skins. Apart from a few combinations from Elementalist, all of Lux’s abilities are either pink, violet, or blue. Ezreal is entirely the same because everything he does; it appears in a bright and dreamy style. They even share a few skin lines, like Star Guardian, Pajama Guardian, and Battle Academy, which gives them the identical look of their spells.

As Ezreal says to Lux: “I’m a mage. You’re a mage. I’m blonde. You’re blonde. Doesn’t it feel like we’re made for each other?”

2. Lux and Ezreal Characters and Lore Are Matching

Anyone who has played both Lux and Ezreal knows why they make for a perfect couple! For starters, they’re both proud characters! Ezreal has grown up without much of his parents and had to do everything by himself, including his gauntlet’s findings. On the other hand, Lux has been forced to do stuff she doesn’t want until she discovered her magic and started building a name for herself. Both of them are mages who’re proud of what they do.

Lux and Ezreal are also one of the most lighthearted and jolly characters in the game! They constantly say fun things and jokes to the player, which really reflects how they approach the fight. Lux beams with positivity and is eager to help in every situation. And Ezreal constantly looks for a way to show off his genius. They both believe in themselves and are self-assured.

But, is this all enough to call them a couple?

1. Lux and Ezreal in Romantic Relationship: Are Lux and Ezreal dating?

Lux and Ezreal’s romantic relationship has been a hot topic for fans since the beginning of the game! Players have fantasized about seeing these two champions in a love theme, but we have no clear answers that they’re really dating! Riot games have never confirmed their relationship, and it’s safest to assume that no, they are not dating. But…

Those who pay extra attention while playing League of Legends can notice the special interactions between Lux and Ezreal. Ezreal says a lot of lines where he hints that he and Lux might be dating:

“Hi, honey! Is it too soon to call you, honey? It’s too soon.”

“I’m sorry, Lux… I love youUUAAAH! I didn’t say that!”

“I’m sparring with Lux?! W-What do I do? Do I fight her?! We have a date later!”

At the same time, Lux responds with a line that isn’t so promising:

“Are we supposed to be… dating… or something? We’ve never even talked.”

So, it might only be wishful thinking on Ezreal’s part. But Zoe is also in love with Ezreal, and she’s got a lot to say to him and Lux:

“I’m not saying Lux isn’t a good girlfriend buuuttt… you could do better!”

“Do you even know he likes you!?”

And if it’s not confirmed that Lux and Ezreal are really dating, then it totally should be! It would shed light on many of these in-game intricacies and it will make them both even more popular! Riot Games have an incredible chance here to satisfy thousands of hungry fans who’re waiting to see these two finally together. We’d love to know how their dates will go…

But what do you think? Do Ezreal and Lux make the perfect LoL couple?
