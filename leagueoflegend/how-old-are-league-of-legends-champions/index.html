      How Old Are League of Legends Champions?

      October 16, 2020, 00:13

      When the “Realms of Runeterra” was released, we got to learn new and exciting things about the LoL champions, such as their age and weight. For those of you who aren’t familiar with “Realms of Runeterra,” this is an encyclopedic-style book that explores League of Legends epic lore. The book was published as a way to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the game. 

      Have you ever wondered how old Amumu was? How about how much Alistar weighs? Continue reading as we take you through a journey of the age and weight of each LoL champion. 

      Take note that the age of the champions comes from the book, but the weight is a concept. Not all of the champions will have their age because “Realms of Runeterra” doesn’t list all the champions. Also, the ages are calculated from 9000BN until 96AN (Before and After Noxus’s foundation).

      Lissandra, Ivern: 8996-9996 years old

      • Ivern – 10’0 and 400 pounds. Looking at his promo art, he’s pretty big, but he isn’t quite tree size. 
      • Lissandra – 6/4 and the weight would be impossible to guess with this champion, because at what point does the ice begin and person end?

      Rek’Sai, Azir, Xerath: 2996-3496 years old

      • Rek’Sai – 30 feet, and the weight is not guessable. 
      • Azir – 8’5 and 400 pounds. 
      • Xerath – 15’. This champion is made of energy, and his weight is not determined. 

      Mordekaiser: 1396-1446 years old

      • Mordekaiser – 12’ and 2-3 tons. 

      Vladimir: 1446-2996 years old

      • Vladimir – 6’3 and 185 pounds. 

      Vel’Koz: 6996-8996 years old

      • Vel’Koz – 30’ and the weight is unclear because, you know, void science is strange. 

      Nasus, Renekton, Rhaast, Zilean, Jax, Varus: 3496-4396 years old

      • Nasus – 9’ and 500 pounds.
      • Renekton – 9’ and 500 pounds as well.
      • Rhaast – 9’ and 400 pounds. 
      • Zilean – 6’1 and 165 pounds. The giant clock he has weighs around 200 pounds, but somehow he manages to make it float.
      • Jax – 6’2 and 145 pounds. If he goes into full Darkin body mode, we can assume he will double in size. 
      • Varus – 5’7 and 145 pounds. 

      Amumu, Rammus, Aatrox – 4396-5996 years old

      • Amumu – 3’7 and 35 pounds. He’s the normal human child-size, but due to mummification, it’s dropped by 15 pounds. 
      • Rammus – 3’0 and 70 pounds. This is based on a big armadillo. 
      • Aatrox – 22’3, and his weight is around two tons.

      Leblanc, Ryze, Zoe, Thresh, Hecarim, Yorick, Kalista: 1021-1396 years old

      • Leblanc – 5’5 and 120 pounds.
      • Ryze – 5’8 and 165 pounds. 
      • Zoe – 4’9 and 85 pounds. 
      • Thresh – 8’ and the weight is unknown. 
      • Hecarim – 9’ and unknown weight. 
      • Yorick – 6’1 and 230 pounds. 
      • Kalista – 5’10, and the weight is unknown.

      Galio: 667-704 years old

      • Galio – 160’ and 4100 tons. 

      Kayle, Nocturne, Morgana, Kled, Poppy: 1021-1396 years old

      • Kayle – 5’6 with a wingspan of 2.4m, and the weight is around 150 pounds. 
      • Nocturne – This is a living nightmare, and we’re not sure of the weight or height. 
      • Morgana – 5’5 with a wingspan of 8’ and a weight of 155 pounds. Her wings weigh about 10 pounds per pair (she has six of those things).
      • Kled – 3’0 and 40 pounds. 
      • Poppy – 3’2 and 50 pounds. Compared to the other champions, she’s tiny, but don’t let that fool you because she’s strong and stocky under that armor.

      Sion, Singed: 81-209 years old

      • Sion – 9’ and 1.5 tons. You know, because metal does weigh a lot.
      • Singed – 6’3 and 140 pounds.

      Swain, Master Yi, Gangplank, Kassadin, Illaoi, Gragas, Malzahar, Udyr, Xin Zhao, Urgot, and Teemo: 41-50 years old

      • Swain – 5’ 11 and 160 pounds. He’s missing half an arm, so his weight went down a tad bit.
      • Master Yi – 5’11 and 160 pounds.
      • Gankplank – 6’0 and 250 pounds.
      • Kassadin – 5’9 and 160 pounds. 
      • Illaoi – 6’5 and 275 pounds.
      • Gragas – 7’2 and 735 pounds.
      • Malzahar – 5’6 and 145 pounds. The floatiness of this LoL champion makes it hard to tell his actual height, so this is a guess. 
      • Udyr – 5’10 and 215 pounds.
      • Xin Zhao – 5’9 and 170 pounds. He could be a bit taller. 
      • Urgot – 10’ and 2000+ pounds.
      • Teemo 2’8″ / 0.81m

      Camille: 80 years old

      • Camille – 6’10, and the weight is unclear.

      Shen, Draven, Zed, Jayce, Viktor, Yasuo, Senna, Lucian, Shen: 33-35 years old

      • Shen – 5’8 and 160 pounds. This really is the beefiest looking ninja we’ve ever seen.
      • Draven – 6’1 and 220 pounds.
      • Zed – 5’10 and 150 pounds.
      • Jayce – 6’0 and 185 pounds.
      • Victor – 6’2 and 280 pounds. You know, robot parts.
      • Yasuo – 5’11 and 170 pounds.
      • Senna – 5’7 and 120 pounds. Senna is slim and athletic. 
      • Luxian – Unknown
      • Shen – Unknown

      Warwick, Lee Sin, Graves, Jhin, Twisted Fate, Darius: 36-40 years old

      • Warwick – 7’8 and 445 pounds.
      • Lee Sin – 5’6, and 155 pounds. Like Bruce Lee, but with even more bulk.
      • Graves – 5’9 and 175 pounds. He has a Dad body.
      • Jhin – 6’4 and 160 pounds. Like a string bean in a way. 
      • Twisted Fate – 6’1 and 150 pounds. He’s on the tall side, but he’s agile and slender.
      • Darius – 6’5 and 266 pounds. 

      Irelia: 24 years old

      • Irelia – 5’8 and 140 pounds. 

      Jinx, Vi: 21 years old

      • Jinx – 5’3 and 98 pounds.

      · Vi – 5’9, and 140 pounds.

      Sejuiani, Taliyah, Ashe: 23 years old

      • Sejuiani – 6’0 and 185 pounds. You don’t want to mess with this champion, trust me.
      • Taliyah – 5’9 and 140 pounds.
      • Ashe – 5’9 and 145 pounds.

      Ekko: 17 years old

      • Ekko – 5’9 and 130 pounds. He may be a tad bit shorter than this.

      Akali: 19 years old

      Akali -5’3 and 110 pounds. Her height comes from her K/DA bio.

      Nunu, Annie: 8 years old

      • Nunu – 4’4 and 63 pounds. Basically, the size of your average eight-year-old boy with some Freljord genes tossed in there.
      • Annie– 4’2 and 58 pounds. The same as Nunu, Annie is the size of your average eight-year-old girl.

      Kai’Sa, Qiyana, Sona, Lux, Orianna, Kayn, Ezreal: 20 years old

      • Kai’Sa – 5’6 and 140 pounds. The height comes from K/DA site, saying she’s under 5’7. The suit she’s wearing is skin-tight, but it contains gauntlets and all of that fun stuff, which is probably 10 pounds.
      • Qiyana – 5’0 and 107 pounds.
      • Sona – 5’6 and 120 pounds. The size of Marilyn Monroe.
      • Lux – 5’10 and 140 pounds. Lux is pretty tall. 
      • Orianna – 5’8 and 170 pounds. This champion could be shorter, but it’s hard to tell, so we’ll say 5’8 to be on the safe side. 
      • Kayn – 6’0 and 145 pounds. He’s much taller than Zed in the comic, but it could be his hair, we’re not sure.
      • Ezreal – 5’8 and 105 pounds. He’s still a teen.

      Miss Fortune, Nidalee, Leona, Shyvana, Jarvan IV, Diana, Taric, Garen, Caitlyn, Riven, Sivir, Cassiopeia, Quinn, Tryndamere, Fiora, Karma, Vayne: 25-31 years old

      • Miss Fortune – 5’6 and 120 pounds. 
      • Nidalee – 5’6 and 120 pounds. She’s an athletic woman.
      • Leona – 6’1 and 195 pounds. This champion is muscular and matronly. She’s a true beast, no doubt, but we’re not walking up to a 6’1 woman and asking what her weight is, so we’ll guess 195 pounds.
      • Shyvana – 5’6 and 140 pounds in human form. In dragon form, 18’ and 6’ long, weighing 1500 pounds.
      • Diana – 5’10 and 150 pounds. She’s on the tiny side, but don’t let this fool you because Diana is pretty strong.
      • Taric – 6’2 and 245 pounds. What you have here is pure masculinity. 
      • Garen – 6’5 and 220 pounds. The armor probably adds around 100 pounds. In-game, his sword is exaggerated and probably weighs 15 pounds.
      • Caitlyn – 5’7 and 125 pounds. Caitlyn has to be fit enough to be able to toss a person to the ground. She seems a bit taller than 5’7. But that’s probably because of her hat and boots.
      • Riven – 5’11 and 160 pounds. She’s almost as tall as Draven.
      • Sivir – 5’8 and 125 pounds. We’re thinking Greek proportions here.
      • Cassiopeia – 13’ and 380 pounds.
      • Quinn – 5’8 and 140 pounds.
      • Tryndamere – 6’1 and 160 pounds. This champion’s right arm is stronger than the left.
      • Fiora – 5’11 and 160 pounds. She may be a bit lighter than this.
      • Karma – 5’8 and 135 pounds. She’s about the same height as Irelia, but she has less muscle mass than her.
      • Vayne – 5’9 and 140 pounds. We heard that tumbling is a great exercise. 

      Conclusion

      It’s always exciting learning about characters in a game, especially when you play the game so much. Personally, we have always wondered about Warwick’s age and weight, and now we know. Which champion surprised you the most?
