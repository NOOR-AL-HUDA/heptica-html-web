      Top 7 Hottest Male League of Legends Champions

      by Davis
      October 18, 2021

      Some time ago, we made a list of the Hottest Female Champions in League of Legends. It is only natural that we make a list about the hottest guys in todays egalitarian world. We just need to give something to the ladies.

      League of Legends is full of good-looking Champions of both sexes. The designs of Champion physiques are a point of admiration and contempt, depending on who you ask. But here at E Games Consult, we dont really care about that. We just want to see a Champ that makes us question things.  

      But not to drag on for too long about unrelated things lets begin with our hot list. 

      Warning: we arent responsible for any sexual tensions you might experience while reading this list. 

      Table of Content show
      7. Pantheon
      6. Braum
      5. Tryndamere
      4. Akshan
      3. Yasuo
      2. Gragas
      1. Viego
      Finishing Thoughts

      7. Pantheon

      Even though we cant see his face, that body is what peak male performance looks like. Pantheon is our favorite Spartan, and we love to admire his looks and vibe whenever we meet him on the Rift. 

      The Spartans were notoriously known for being fierce and robust warriors, and Pantheon is no exception. Yes, I know hes not actually a Spartan, but the inspiration is so blatant that I never differentiate between the two. 

      Some of his skins, i.e., Perseus and Glaive Warrior, give us a look at his face, showing us just how good-looking he is under that helmet. Ladies beware, this flaming spear-wielder might be too hot to touch. 

      6. Braum

      Did I say Pantheon was peak male performance? No, I was thinking of Braum. Now, this is what I see as the perfect physique. In contrast to the fiery warrior previously, this ice-cold hunk of Freljord meat is a real treat for the ladies. And theres plenty for everyone.

      That mustache also makes me jealous, apart from other things. Sometimes, I cry myself to sleep wishing for such facial hair, but alas. 

      His skin Santa Braum shows us a body that most of us can only dream of. I think it might be time to hit the gym and lift. Its our only way bros. 

      Also read: 7 Best 1v1 Champions in League of Legends

      5. Tryndamere

      Staying in Freljord, we move on to our sword-swinging maniac known as Tryndamere. Though he might be almost borderline insane and immortal, hes just as breathtaking. Just looking at him makes you feel things you never felt before. 

      His right arm is also stronger than his left arm, if you know what I mean, ladies. 

      The Freljord characters all seem to have amazing bodies, making me wonder what exactly is up with that place. Perhaps its the fact that the whole area is perpetually covered in ice and snow, so the gym is the only place to go. I have no other explanation. 

      At the end of the day, theyre all hot as hell, likely helping them stay warm during those storms.

      4. Akshan

      One of the more recent additions to the League of Legends Champion pool is this necromancing menace. Akshan has that Mediterranean vibe all around him, and we just know how good-looking Med men are. 

      Despite how sexy he is, I hate this Champion. Ever since I returned to League after a short hiatus, I ban him permanently. There hasnt been a single Champion Select screen that I havent banned Akshan in. It doesnt matter what game mode Im playing if theres a ban option, I will ban him. 

      Ive only had this happen to me with a few Champions, namely Akali and some others. I dont like her, but she is also a treat to look at am I banning only hot Champs?

      3. Yasuo

      Yet another Champion that I despise yet has fantastic looks. This is something that Riot does; it seems to make some of the Champs harder to hate. This Japanese master of the wind has that perfect build that everyone would like to have. 

      Not too buffed, yet not frail either. If this list doesnt make you want to start lifting, I dont know what will. It is vital to keep a healthy lifestyle parallel to playing League (or video games in general). 

      If you balance it out perfectly, youll have both a good physique and rank high on the League of Legends ladders. Best of both worlds. 

      Also read: 10 Best Teamfight Champions in League of Legends

      2. Gragas

      All women love dad bods, and yes, Gragas has one. He represents a future we are all going towards if we dont pay enough attention to some things. But, then again, is it really that bad to drink wine all day and have fun? 

      Likely not, but its far from reality too. 

      At any rate, Gragas gives us that heavy strongman vibe, and I just think hed be able to carry an entire ship on his back. I wouldnt make him try, though. 

      1. Viego

      Our edgy King of Death is genuinely every femboys wet dream. Lean, mean, and killing machine, Viego looks the part for his role of dominating over everyone that he deems beneath him. 

      I dont know how he manages to keep up the youth for so long. He must let us know what he uses to stop his aging process! The doctors must hate him, though. 

      Viego makes us jealous of his physique, and he obliterates us in the Rift. We just dont know how to deal with this rollercoaster of emotions whenever we meet him. Viego, please have our children. 

      Finishing Thoughts

      I always have fun writing articles that are not in some serious tone. Nothing in this article should be taken to heart. Its all a big joke, after all. We admire male bodies and female ones just the same. We must have some fun from time to time, both in the game and outside of it, because without it, theres really no point in anything.

      We love to hear your thoughts and your jokes, so dont miss out on throwing some out there.
