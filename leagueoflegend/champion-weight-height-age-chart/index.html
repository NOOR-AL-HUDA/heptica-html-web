      League of Legends Champions’ Weight, Height, and Age Chart (Updated 2022)

      by Davis
      January 1, 2022

      When the “Realms of Runeterra” was released, we got to learn new and exciting things about the LoL champions, such as their age and weight. For those of you who aren’t familiar with “Realms of Runeterra,” this is an encyclopedic-style book that explores League of Legends epic lore. The book was published as a way to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the game. 

      Have you ever wondered how old Amumu was? How about how much Alistar weighs? Continue reading as we take you through a journey of the age and weight of each LoL champion. 

      Take note that the age of the champions comes from the book, but the weight is a concept. Not all of the champions will have their age because “Realms of Runeterra” doesn’t list all the champions. Also, the ages are calculated from 9000BN until 96AN (Before and After Noxus’s foundation).
