10 Most Appealing Female League of Legends Champions of 2020

September 11, 2020, 14:34

In this post, we will give the spotlight to the female League of Legends characters because we feel they deserve it. Curious who the most appealing LoL female champions of 2020 may be? Is your main on this list? Continue reading to find out…

Last time we counted, there were a little over 32 female champions in League of Legends, and while each of the champions has their own unique design, there are ten that really stood out from the crowd of the rest. 

Keep in mind that these female LoL champions are not listed in any particular order…

10) Irelia – the Blade Dancer

I remember back in 2010 when Irelia was released – as soon as she emerged, I had to give her a try. In Top lane, Irelia just so happens to be one of the most popular melee champions, and she has that sweet innocent look to her, so it’d be crazy not to include her on this list. Eight years after her release, Irelia went through a rework phase, and from there, she became even more popular with her practical stun and dash kit. 

Irelia helped defeat the Noxian invaders, and for this reason, she’s a hero of Ionia. To the Ionian people, Irelia is symbolic. 

9) Morgana – The Fallen

Morgana first walked on the LoL stage 11 years ago, in 2009, and to date, she is one of the most popular mid and support champions in League of Legends. She’s Kayle’s sister and the daughter of the Aspect of justice. There was a point in time when Morgana was a guardian of Demacia. I know many people that use Morgana as their main on League of Legends, and I used to be one of those people.  

8) Fiora – The Grand Duelist

Fiora is another champion that was released years ago, in 2012 and is a well-known melee champion in the top lane, sitting right next to Irelia. Fiora is head of the Demacian noble house of House Laurent. 

Have you ever looked at Fiora’s skins? If so, then you probably already know why we included her on this list of the most appealing LoL female champions of 2020 – her most attractive skins, in our opinion, would be Soaring Sword, Project, Nightraven, Headmistress, and of course, Pool Party. While Fiora is attractive, Riot Games didn’t set out to sexualize her champion design or skin. 

7) Zyra – Rise of the Thorns

Zyra was also released in 2012 – her beautiful flower design turns many heads. Zyra is a species of a carnivorous flower – a flower that a sorceress destroyed during a magical cataclysm. However, after those flowers were destroyed from the grounds, they were reborn and today, they walk the earth in a humanoid form – one we know as Zyra, an attractive LoL champion.

Looking at the champion design, Zyra is similar to Poison Ivy (from DC)– we think of her as the LoL version of Poison Ivy. Poison Ivy is a supervillain that many of us are familiar with in DC Comics. She is a Gotham City biochemist and botanist who has an obsession over ecological extinction, plants, and environmentalism. 

6) Katarina – The Sinister Blade

Sitting beside our beautiful Morgana, Katarina is another appealing LoL champion that was released in 2009. Katarina is a well-known assassin in League and a member of the Noxian noble house of Du Couteau. She also just so happens to be Cassiopeia’s sister and a kick-butt assassin for the Noxian military. 

5) Jinx – the Loose Cannon

Jinx is a female champion introduced in 2013, and today, she’s still going strong as one of the most popular Marksmen/AD-Carries in LoL. 

Despite having a flat chest, she’s still a beautiful character, and there’s just something about her look. Perhaps her attractiveness isn’t just because of how she looks, but her spontaneous personality. When we think of Jinx, we think pf Harley Quinn from DC comics. 

4) Kindred – The Eternal Hunters

Kindred is an anthropomorphic animal character, but it has human characteristics and personalities, and we just had to include it on this list. It was released five years ago, in 2015, and in season 6 all the way to season 8; it was one of the hottest picks. It’s a strong LoL champion early to mid-game, but unfortunately, we can’t find many players that choose it anymore. We believe this could be because of its statistics combined with her marksman skillset. For whatever reason, we still had to include Kindred on this list of the most appealing LoL female champions of 2020 because we feel it deserves some love from the LoL community. 

3) Evelynn – Agony’s Embrace

Evelynn is one of the first LoL champions out. She is a demon with a seductive side that she uses to lure people in…and I must say, she does a surprisingly good job at it – I mean look, she somehow made her way on this list. Misery loves company, and she feeds off of people’s pain and misery. In order to continue to live, she continues to torture others. Her character design, combined with her bio, makes it obvious why she is on this list in the first place. 

2) Riven – The Exile

For many years now, for Top lane, Riven has been at the top of many people’s lists. She made her debut in 2011 and took the League of Legends community by storm. She is a soldier with total faith in Noxus, but currently, she can be found in Ionia, looking for redemption in her own exile. 

1) Ahri – The Nine-Tailed Fox

Last on our list; we have Ahri, another popular female pick in League of Legends. This champion was also released in 2011 and is a Vastaya from Iona. Ahri fed on others’ emotions and memories, and then in the process, she killed them. When we think of Ahri, we think of D.VA from Overwatch because she’s just as popular as her.

Conclusion

The most appealing LoL female champions of 2020 that we have included on this list aren’t just based on our opinions – we conducted research, gathered data, and discovered what female champions people adore the most. 

What about you? What female League of Legend champion are you the most thankful for? Do you feel there’s another female champion that deserves her spotlight on this list? If so, please drop her name in the comment section below, and tell us why you chose her.
